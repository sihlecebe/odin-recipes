Chef John's Homemade Chicken Noodle Soup

This homemade chicken noodle soup is soul-warming and deliciously simple - just chicken and noodles

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 cup diced carrot
  • 1/2 cup diced onion
  • 1/2 cup diced celery
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 2 tablespoons melted chicken fat

Step needed to make the dish

  1. Melt butter in a large soup pot over medium heat
  2. Stir in roasted chicken broth and bring to a boil
  3. Stir in egg noodles; cook until tender, about 5 minutes
  4. Add cooked chicken breast meat; simmer until heated thrrough, about 5 minutes.
